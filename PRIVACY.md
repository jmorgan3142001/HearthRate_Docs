# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 30, 2025

## Introduction

HearthRate is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform. We comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

-   Name, email address, and phone number
-   Real estate license number and brokerage information
-   Profile photo and bio
-   Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

### Usage Data

-   Tour and event data (properties, ratings, feedback)
-   Lead capture information (visitor contact details, preferences)
-   Decision deck data (property comparisons, offer scenarios)
-   Device information (browser type, IP address, operating system)
-   Analytics data (pages visited, time spent, feature usage)

### Offline Data

HearthRate uses offline-first architecture, storing data locally in your browser's IndexedDB. This data is synchronized with our servers when you're online and is deleted when you clear your browser data.

## How We Use Your Information

-   Provide and maintain our service
-   Process your transactions and manage your subscription
-   Send you important updates, security alerts, and administrative messages
-   Respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests
-   Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform
-   Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
-   Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following situations:

-   **With your consent:** When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
-   **Service providers:** Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
-   **Business transfers:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
-   **Legal requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights
-   **Client data:** Tour and event data may be shared with household members via access links

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

-   **Access:** Request a copy of all your data (available in Settings)
-   **Rectification:** Update or correct your personal information
-   **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
-   **Portability:** Export your data in a machine-readable format (JSON)
-   **Opt-out:** Unsubscribe from marketing emails
-   **Object:** Object to certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, visit your Account Settings or contact us at [privacy@hearthrate.app](mailto:privacy@hearthrate.app).

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. When you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where we must retain certain information to comply with legal obligations.

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

-   Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
-   Encrypted data storage
-   JWT-based authentication
-   Regular security audits
-   Access controls and monitoring

## Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies and local storage to:

-   Maintain your login session
-   Remember your preferences
-   Enable offline functionality (IndexedDB)

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Analytics data is anonymized.

## Children's Privacy

HearthRate is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or through a prominent notice on our platform. Your continued use of HearthRate after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
